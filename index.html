About me

I am a Lecturer/Assistant Professor in Electrical Power Systems within the School of Electrical & Electronic Engineering at University College Dublin.  My research focuses on the development of novel techno-economic concepts, methods and tools for cyber-physical electric power system planning and operation under uncertainty.
