About me

I am a Lecturer/Assistant Professor in Electrical Power Systems within the School of Electrical & Electronic Engineering at University College Dublin.  My research focuses on the development of novel techno-economic concepts, methods and tools for electric power system planning and operation under uncertainty. My interests and expertise include:

  • Electric power system cyber-physical risk, reliability and resilience management.
  • Multi-TSO coordination.
  • Power system economics & electricity markets.
  • Stochastic optimisation.
  • Artificial intelligence & Machine learning.
  • Quantum computing.
