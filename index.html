About me

I am a Lecturer/Assistant Professor in Electrical Power Systems within the School of Electrical & Electronic Engineering at University College Dublin.  My research focuses on the development of novel techno-economic concepts, methods and tools for electric power system planning and operation under uncertainty. My interests include:

  • Electric power system cyber-physical risk,reliability and resilience management.
  • Multi-TSO coordination.
  • Power system economics & electricity market.
  • Stochastic optimisation.
  • Artificial Intelligence & Machine-Learning.
  • Quantum Computing.
